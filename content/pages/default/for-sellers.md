You've spent years building something real. A business that supports families, serves customers, and reflects who you are. Now you're thinking about what comes next.

That moment — the decision about what happens to what you built — is the most important business decision you'll ever make.

We understand what you're going through.

## Three Things Every Seller Feels

**Fear:** Will my employees be taken care of? Will my customers still get great service? Will my name still mean something good?

**Relief:** Someone who won't flip my business in 3 years. Someone who actually knows how to run a company. Someone who won't make promises they can't keep.

**Hope:** A partner who shares my pride in what I built. Someone who sees the potential I always knew was there. A chance for my legacy to outlive me.

## Why Sellers Choose Kairos

**Long-Term Ownership** — No short-term flip. No breakup. No financial engineering. We acquire businesses to own and grow them for the long term.

**Hands-On Operators** — We're not absentee investors. We roll up our sleeves and run the businesses we acquire.

**Straightforward Deals** — Clear terms, low friction, high integrity. We say what we mean and do what we say.

## What We Look For

We acquire founder-led and family-owned businesses in Georgia (expanding regionally) with $500K–$3M+ in annual EBITDA. We focus on home services, B2B services, specialty trades, light industrial, and logistics businesses with loyal customer bases and recurring revenue.

## What the Process Looks Like

1. **A simple conversation** — No NDA required to start. Just a candid call to see if there's a fit.
2. **A clear offer** — If it makes sense, we move quickly to a fair, straightforward Letter of Intent.
3. **Focused diligence** — We review what we need to — nothing more.
4. **A clean close** — You get paid. We work together on a transition that protects your people and customers.

Most deals close in 60 days or less.

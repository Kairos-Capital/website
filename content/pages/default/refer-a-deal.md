Know a business owner thinking about selling? We'd love an introduction.

We work with a small network of trusted professionals who refer business owners our way. If you send us a deal that closes, we compensate you for the relationship.

## Who We're Looking For

We acquire founder-led and family-owned businesses with:

- $500K–$3M+ in annual EBITDA
- Based in Georgia (expanding regionally)
- Industries: home services, B2B services, specialty trades, light industrial, logistics
- Loyal customer base and recurring or repeat revenue
- Owner ready to transition

## Who Makes Great Referral Partners

We work closely with the professionals already trusted by business owners:

- **CPAs and accountants** — You know your clients' financial picture and succession concerns better than anyone
- **Business brokers** — We're a reliable, relationship-focused buyer who closes deals and treats sellers well
- **Estate and business attorneys** — Succession planning conversations happen in your office
- **Financial advisors** — You counsel clients on liquidity events and wealth transitions
- **Industry associations and peer networks** — You know who's thinking about their next chapter

## How It Works

If you refer a business owner who becomes a successful acquisition, we compensate you for the introduction. Referral terms are discussed directly — reach out and we'll have a straightforward conversation about what that looks like.

We handle every introduction with professionalism and confidentiality. Your client will never feel handed off to a faceless fund.
